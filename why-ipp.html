Why Choose IPP

Reliability isn't a claim here — it's measured.

Manufacturing Capability

Twenty-five years of continuous investment — from a single blown-film extrusion line in 1999 to a multi-line, rotogravure-printing, advanced-pouching facility today — means IPP can take a packaging brief from concept to full production run without outsourcing any core step.

Quality Standards

Our Jebel Ali facility is BRC certified, and every run is tested for seal strength, barrier performance and print registration before dispatch. That discipline has been recognised externally too, with multiple FPA Flexible Packaging Achievement Awards and a Dow Packaging Innovation Platinum award.

Innovation

In-house R&D works directly with brand and machinery partners — the kind of collaboration that produced our award-winning recyclable rice pouch, and that continues to add specialised features like laser scoring and anti-fog coatings to our standard capability.

Customer Relationships

We track On-Time-In-Full (OTIF) delivery against every purchase order, and assign a dedicated account team from first sample through to repeat production — the goal is a packaging partner you don't have to manage closely, not just a vendor.
