Company Profile

A manufacturing division of the Lutfi Group of Companies, building flexible packaging in Dubai since 1999.

Manufacturing since 1999

Integrated Plastics Packaging is a manufacturing division of the Lutfi Group of Companies, established in 1999 in the National Industries Park, Jebel Ali Free Zone, Dubai — United Arab Emirates.

We manufacture and convert customised, high-quality flexible packaging materials, primarily for the food industry, and also for hygiene, personal care, pharmaceutical, cosmetic, fertilizer and construction segments. We are a packaging market leader, with experience working with leading global brands such as Lipton, Brooke Bond, Dettol, McVitie's, Tang, Cadbury, Maggi, Mackintosh, Tiffany, Igloo, Nando's, Emirates, and many more.

We value investing in people, purpose-built infrastructure, world-reputed machines, the highest safety and hygiene standards, and strategic supplier partnerships. At IPP we focus on bespoke print and reproduction quality and solutions for each client and their brand value.

Our "can-do attitude" culture, built upon a unified set of values, fuels our passion for motivation, learning, development and sustainability. All packaging materials from IPP help our customers' products stand out at point of sale.

Market Position & Industries Served

IPP serves food, beverage, healthcare, personal & household care and pet food manufacturers — supplying six flexible-packaging categories and eight specialised film technologies from a single BRC-certified facility.

25+ Years of Growth

From one extrusion line to a full-service converter.

1999Established in Dubai with a Blown Film PE Co-Extrusion Plant
2000Started Flexo (Stack) printing press
2001Started lamination and converting rolls into bags and pouches
2004Started Flexo (C.I) printing press
2008Relocated to a purpose-built BRC/IoP site; transformed from Flexo to Rotogravure printing
2011Major capacity expansion
2015–2017Capacity enhanced to 12,600 MT/PA with new machines and equipment
2018Advanced pouching capabilities added; plant upgraded
2020Added two pouching lines — 3D and CS
2021Added special pouch features: coffee valves, spouts, embossing
2022Introduced laser scoring — linear and shaped
2023Delivering sustainable, recyclable laminates and pouches
