Inside the Factory

Eight stages, one continuous line.

Scroll to follow a roll of film from raw resin to a dispatched, print-perfect pouch.

01

Resin Intake

PE, PP and PET granules are quality-checked and blended to spec.

02

Extrusion

Blown-film lines cast the base substrate to precise micron tolerances.

03

Rotogravure Printing

Up to 10-colour printing reproduces brand artwork with exact registration.

04

Lamination

Layers are bonded for barrier, strength and shelf appeal.

05

Slitting

Master rolls are cut to the exact width each production line needs.

06

Pouching

Film is formed, sealed and fitted with zippers, spouts or valves.

07

Quality Control

Seal strength, barrier and print are tested against BRC standards.

08

Dispatch

Orders are palletised and routed through our global export network.
