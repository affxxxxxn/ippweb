Beverages

Overview

IPP manufactures flexible packaging for powdered beverages, coffee, tea, juices, water and carbonated soft drinks — engineered to protect aroma, carbonation and moisture sensitivity across the supply chain.

Powdered BeveragesCoffeeTeaJuicesWaterCarbonated Soft Drinks

Applications

  • Fresh-roast and instant coffee pouches with degassing valves
  • Loose-leaf and sachet tea packs with aroma barrier
  • Juice and powdered-drink stand-up pouches with spouts
  • Water sachets and flexible pouch formats
  • Carbonated soft drink flexible packs

Materials & Technical Notes

Structures typically combine PET/foil or metallized laminates for aroma and oxygen barrier, with PE sealant layers. Coffee formats commonly include a one-way degassing valve; juice and water formats use spouted or heat-seal closures.

Benefits

  • Locks in aroma for coffee and tea
  • Protects against moisture ingress
  • Supports high-speed filling lines
  • Available in recyclable mono-material options
