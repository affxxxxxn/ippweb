Innovation & Technology

State-of-the-art machinery and continuous process innovation, from resin to finished pouch.

Manufacturing Timeline

A 25-year investment in capability.

2000

Flexo (Stack) Printing

First printing press installed a year after founding.

2001

Lamination & Converting

Rolls converted into bags and pouches in-house.

2004

Flexo (C.I) Printing

Central-impression flexo press added.

2008

Rotogravure & BRC Site

Relocated to a purpose-built BRC/IoP facility; upgraded to rotogravure.

2018

Advanced Pouching

Plant upgraded with advanced pouching capability.

2021

R&D

Innovation is key to creating value.

At IPP, innovation is central to creating value for customers, stakeholders, employees and business partners. The company offers customised solutions for any packaging development brief, backed by state-of-the-art machinery to attain exceptional quality.

This shows up across eight specialised product features — Anti-fog Film/Coating, High Burst Strength Packaging, Laser Scoring, Matt/Gloss Effect Coatings, Perforated Films, Recyclable Packaging, Slider Zipper and Tactile Varnish — each developed to solve a specific customer or shelf-presence challenge.
